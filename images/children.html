CHILDREN'S SPORT AND MEDICAL SERVICES

Mission has organized sports competition for over 124 indigent kids from five rural towns in Ekiti to develop their skills in sports and particularly soccer. Over 500 children participated in the event. Children that needed medical care received free medical attention during the program. Each of them received a jersey, soccer shoes, a soccer ball, and Mercy t-shirt. The children, their parents, and teachers were very excited about the program. Our supporting communities in Minnesota (Chaska school District and other communities) greatly helped make this program possible by providing the soccer balls and other sports equipment Part of our goal with this program is to promote sports development among the youth. The event was held at the Osi Community School premises in Osi-Ekiti in Ido-Osi Local Government Area of Ekiti State.
