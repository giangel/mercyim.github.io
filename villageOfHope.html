MERCY INT'L VILLAGE OF HOPE

The "Village of Hope" located on 35 acres of donated land in Osi-Ekiti is a one-stop medical Mercy International Village of Hope campus designed to provide access to medical services in one location. The "Village" will include a Medical Center, Community Center, Guest House, Garden, Sports areas, and a Morgue. Development of Healthcare delivery in many of the small towns and villages in rural Ekiti state, and in Nigeria, is known to be fraught with challenges. Some of these include interrupted and non-functional electricity, water and sanitation problems, maintenance of an adequate supply of pharmaceuticals and lab equipment, transportation (poor roads), and human safety. Mercy International Mission (MIM) has addressed many of these challenges through the various projects our organization has taken on since our inception in 2007. We have served this area using a 3-pronged approach including 1) stationary clinic, 2) mobile health van, and 3) annual large-scale healthcare events. The mobile clinic operates in Ekiti-state and has provided healthcare services in over 20 different towns and villages. The mobile clinic is able to provide basic health screening for common chronic and infectious diseases (such as diabetes mellitus, hypertension/high blood pressure, and malaria) and simple common treatments and common medications. The mobile clinic seeks to bring medical care to the doorsteps of people who would normally not be able to access such care due to cost or availability.

Village of Hope

Health Clinic

Our medical Centre host's our annual medical mission's. Since inception we have reached over 50,000 person with regards provision of free healthcare services at absolutely no cost.

Pharmacy

Our pharmacy maintains an inventory of medication needed for administration during and after our medical missions at no cost to residents and members of the community.

Diagnostic Centre

The diagnostic center when completed will be used for tests and procedures to diagnose various medical conditions and illnesses it will also be used for research purposes.

Guest House

Our Guest House currently accommodates Missionaries and volunteers, patients and their families waiting for lab results, medical professionals and women waiting to give birth.

Community Centre

Our community center when completed will be utilized for townhall meetings and communal activities which might be recreational, educational or leisurely.

Morgue

The Morgue when completed will serve as storage for human corpses awaiting identification, autopsy, burial, cremation or other methods of disposal.
