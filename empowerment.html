Empowerment Initiatives

Mercy International Mission provides small business loans to youths and women in the Local community to establish farming operations, start or grow their small businesses and provide for their families. The aim of this program is to encourage self help by putting people's destiny in their hands and transforming their communities. As a way of supporting participants of this initiative, we organize a monthly support group meeting to support their growth and address challenges facing them.

Mercy International Mission also currently provides scholarship to two gifted and talented students from poor families to further their education, each scholarship amount is equivalent to $50. The scholarship is meant to pay their college entrance examination and expenses.

Our entrepreneurship program for the youths includes digital and vocational skills training, business mentorship programs and startup grants for immediate translation of skills to viable businesses for self sustenance and sustainability.
