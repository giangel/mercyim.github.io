Welcome to Mercy International Mission

Mercy International Mission, USA and Mercy International Mission, Nigeria exist to reduce disease and mortality rates in southwestern Nigeria through the provision of essential medical care, preventive health services, and health education; to strengthen local communities by facilitating self-development activities; and to improve overall quality of life for men, women, and children in the region.

OUR SERVICES

Mercy International Village of Hope

A comprehensive medical campus to address healthcare challenges in rural Ekiti State, Nigeria.

Outpatient Clinic

We operate a permanent outpatient clinic in Osi-Ekiti.

Mercy Annual Medical Mission

The Annual Medical Mission takes place annually over 4 days. During this event, volunteer doctors, nurses and others serve people from Osi-Ekiti and the 21 surrounding villages.

Mercy Guest House

The guest house-a wing of the facility used for housing patients and their families and visiting staff, especially during the medical mission.

Mobile Clinic

Our mobile clinics and base office in Ekiti provide comprehensive healthcare to rural and vulnerable populations.

Telemedicine Service

We have engaged the services of A-Vu Media technology services company focused on providing simplified access to broadband-enabled telemedicine in the United States and Africa.

Mercy International Medical Clinic

A full-service clinic designed to serve the rural villages of Southwest Nigeria.

OUR ACTIVITIES

COMMUNITY OUTREACH AND COUNSELING

The annual medical mission outreach consists of medical services, community outreach, and spiritual counseling.

YOUTH EMPOWERMENT

We continued to provide the necessary support to ten men and women in the local community who previously received empowerment loans to start or grow their small businesses.

CHILDREN'S SPORT AND MEDICAL SERVICES

Mission has organized sports competition for over 124 indigent kids from five rural towns in Ekiti to develop their skills in sports, particularly soccer.

COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT

In addition to our main vision of providing access to basic medical care services, Mercy International Mission is also contributing to the development of the host village, Osi-Ekiti.
