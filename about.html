About Us

Mercy International Mission

Mercy International Mission (Village of Hope) is a registered Non-Governmental Organization with presence in Nigeria and the United States with the mission to reduce disease and mortality rate in Nigeria especially in Rural and under served communities. Our Vision was conceived following the visit of the founders to their native town in Nigeria noticing the high decadence of education system poor health services, extreme poverty and Nigeria's life expectancy (52) years against Minnesota's life expectancy of 81 years where they reside.

The organization is poised to foster a brighter future by caring for health and wellbeing of people through the provision of essential medical care with support for basic hygiene, food preparation and infection prevention; strengthening local communities by facilitating self development programs and improving livelihood of youth and the vulnerable population. Our village of hope is designed to house a functional clinic, laboratory, pharmacy, community center and a library to meet the needs of Osi-Ekiti and its environs.

At Mercy International Mission we believe that everyone in the world should have access to quality and affordable healthcare services. The reality in rural communities drives our team to develop and implement innovative solutions to this basic challenge. Understanding the links to rural health, we develop programs that address major drivers of rural health and marginalization with priority to Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH) facilities, food security and nutrition, health education, outpatient services, sport and skill empowerment.

Our Primary focus is on children, elderly, adolescent and vulnerable women because of their lack of access to affordable healthcare services and their inability to meet essential basic healthcare need. We remain committed to providing high quality health care service for a sustainable and healthy world.
