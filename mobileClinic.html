MOBILE CLINIC

The mobile clinic team consisting of staff and volunteers traveled several miles to cover twelve villages, spread across four Local Government areas (Counties), in very difficult terrains in fulfillment of the goal of providing compassionate, holistic and cost effective healthcare to the poor and vulnerable people of rural Ekiti. The same goes for the Out-Patient clinic at the base office, whose reputation continues to spread, as a place where qualitative and efficacious healthcare is provided. Mercy International operates mobile vans that travel from village to village providing medical screenings and interventions to the people of Osi-Ekiti and its surrounding communities within a 15km radius. In our mobile van, patients were treated for hypertension, malarial fever, diabetes, osteoarthritis, and gastroenteritis. In this catchment area, the most prevalent conditions treated were hypertension, malaria fever, and osteoarthritis.
