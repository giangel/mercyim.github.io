2021 Road Side Clearing Exercise

As part of Mercy International Mission, we identified the needs for community service.

Road side clearing is one of the major service we offer to identified communities in the rural areas.

In our study we believed road network is important for improved economy, a good and cleared road also reduces the rate of auto crashes and reduce criminal activities in the local level.
