2022 Medical Mission at EKiti-Oke

2022 Free Medical Mission

Mercy International Mission 2022 medical mission was a 3-day even covering comprehensive medical services for people living in Ekiti State and across Nigeria. Individuals with pressing medical and surgical needs were top of our priorities especially those who could not afford the exorbitant medical bills for surgical procedures. General consultation, free medications; basic and uncomplicated surgeries; dental services; eye screening, mental counselling; hypertensive and diabetic screening covered the scope of our operations. We also advise intending beneficiaries to pre-register at Mercy Cybercafé between September 19th and 24th, 2022 to help us project the number of expected beneficiaries and provide accordingly.
